Infos

Bitcoin is the world's first decentralized cryptocurrency that enables peer-to-peer electronic cash transactions without intermediaries like banks or governments. Bitcoin operates on a blockchain secured by proof-of-work mining and the SHA-256 cryptographic algorithm, making it virtually impossible to counterfeit or double-spend. With a fixed supply cap of 21 million coins and programmatic halvings every four years, Bitcoin is designed as a deflationary digital asset often called "digital gold." Bitcoin has achieved mainstream adoption through spot ETF approvals, corporate treasury holdings, and acceptance by governments and institutions worldwide.
